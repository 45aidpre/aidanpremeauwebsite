Natural Computing Algorithms

Intro to Natural Computing was a course I took at Florida Gulf Coast University concerning how nature influences some algorithms used in coding and animation. Throughout this course I learned many different methods including but not limited to Boids algorithm, Lindenmayer Systems and Cellular automation. Many of these concern the imitation of natural occurences through simulations. The Boid is simply a particle that follows a particular set of predefined parameters dictating its movement and how it interacts with other particles and space. Most notibly, particle systems like this one are used to simulate flocks of birds in animations like in movies.
